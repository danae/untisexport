Untis Export

Download your WebUntis schedule for use in Outlook, iCalendar and Google Calendar in two simple steps.

Connect to WebUntis

Enter the credentials to connect to your WebUntis-service.

This is the hostname part of the WebUntis URL (e.g. mese.webuntis.com)

Download your schedule

Select the schedules you want to download in an iCalendar file.

Use CTRL-click or SHIFT-click to select multiple classes at once

Please note that generating the iCalendar file can take a little while.
